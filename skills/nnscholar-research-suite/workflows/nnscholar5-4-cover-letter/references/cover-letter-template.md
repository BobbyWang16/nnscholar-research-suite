# Cover Letter Template

```markdown
Dear [Editor Title and Name],

We are pleased to submit our manuscript, "[Title]", as a [article type] for consideration in [Venue].

[One paragraph: research problem and why it matters.]

[One paragraph: main contribution and strongest supported findings.]

[One paragraph: why this venue is a good fit.]

We confirm that [originality/no concurrent submission/ethics/COI/data availability declarations as appropriate].

Thank you for considering our manuscript.

Sincerely,

[Corresponding author]
```

## Audit

- Specific to target venue, not generic.
- Contribution matches manuscript evidence.
- Declarations are true and author-confirmed.
- Tone is professional and concise.
