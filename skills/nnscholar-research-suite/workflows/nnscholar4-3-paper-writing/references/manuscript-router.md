# Manuscript Router

Use this file to choose the manuscript family before drafting.

## Universal Questions

1. Is this a result paper, review, methods paper, benchmark paper, case report, thesis chapter, proposal, or grant-style document?
2. Is there a locked protocol or only a concept?
3. Are real results available, or should the draft use planned/placeholder language?
4. Are figures and tables already generated, planned, or missing?
5. What is the target audience: domain specialists, interdisciplinary readers, reviewers, thesis committee, funders, or conference committee?

## Article Families

| Family | Drafting structure | Evidence requirement |
|---|---|---|
| Empirical IMRaD paper | Abstract, Introduction, Methods, Results, Discussion | Locked methods and at least provisional results |
| Clinical original article | Structured abstract, Introduction, Methods, Results, Discussion | Cohort, endpoint, statistics, ethics placeholders |
| AI benchmark paper | Abstract, Introduction, Related Work, Method, Experiments, Results, Discussion | Dataset, baselines, metrics, implementation |
| Materials/chemistry paper | Abstract, Introduction, Experimental, Characterization, Performance, Discussion | Synthesis, characterization, testing conditions |
| Education/psychology study | Abstract, Introduction, Methods, Results, Discussion | Participants, instruments, reliability, ethics |
| Economics/social science | Abstract, Introduction, Data, Empirical Strategy, Results, Robustness, Discussion | Data, identification, model specifications |
| Humanities article | Abstract, Introduction, Corpus/Sources, Analytical Sections, Discussion | Corpus/source logic and interpretive evidence |
| Review/scoping review | Abstract, Introduction, Search/Selection, Thematic Synthesis, Gaps, Conclusion | Search strategy and inclusion logic |
| Proposal/grant | Summary, Background, Aims, Approach, Expected Outcomes, Risks | Hypothesis and feasible plan, not actual results |

## Default Selection

- If 2.2 ARS and 2.3 architecture exist: use the article family selected by 2.3.
- If only hypothesis and plan exist: draft a proposal-style or planned-study manuscript, not a fake results paper.
- If 4.1-4.4 outputs exist: use them to structure Results and figure/table callouts.
