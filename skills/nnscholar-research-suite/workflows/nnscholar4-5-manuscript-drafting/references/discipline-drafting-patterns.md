# Discipline Drafting Patterns

## Clinical Medicine

Use restrained causal language unless trial design supports causality. Methods should include design, setting, eligibility, exposure/intervention, endpoints, statistics, missing data, ethics placeholder, and validation if relevant.

Results should move from cohort flow and baseline table to primary outcome, secondary outcomes, subgroup/sensitivity, and safety or clinical utility.

## Basic Biomedicine

Methods should describe model system, samples, reagents, assays, replicates, normalization, controls, and statistics.

Results should separate representative images from quantification and avoid claiming mechanism from correlation alone.

## AI / Data Science

Use clear experiment reporting: datasets, splits, preprocessing, models, baselines, metrics, implementation, hardware, seeds/folds, uncertainty, and ablations.

Results should distinguish primary benchmark, ablation, robustness, error analysis, and deployment/interpretability.

## Materials / Chemistry

Methods/Experimental should report synthesis, composition, characterization, instrument conditions, testing protocols, and repetitions.

Results should connect structure/composition to properties and performance without overclaiming mechanism.

## Education / Psychology

Methods should report participants, recruitment, intervention, instruments, scale range/direction, reliability, time points, analysis, attrition, and ethics.

Results should present baseline equivalence, primary outcomes, secondary outcomes, mediation/moderation, and sensitivity analyses.

## Economics / Social Science

Methods may be Data and Empirical Strategy. Distinguish identification assumptions from descriptive analysis. Report fixed effects, controls, clustering, sample restrictions, and robustness.

Results should move from descriptive statistics to main estimates, mechanisms, heterogeneity, and robustness.

## Humanities

Do not force IMRaD. Draft around argument progression, sources/corpus, interpretive framework, close reading/coding logic, and counterargument handling.

## Engineering

Methods should report system design, implementation, environment, workload, baselines, metrics, repetitions, and failure/safety testing.

Results should distinguish performance, robustness, scalability, ablation, and failure mode analysis.
